Current Events

We are excited to announce the upcoming Maize Harvest Festival, scheduled for July 15th at Cross River's Agricultural Hub. Organized by the Cross River Maize Chamber of Commerce, this festival aims to celebrate the region's rich agricultural heritage and promote sustainable farming practices. The event will feature farmer showcases, interactive workshops on modern maize cultivation techniques, and networking opportunities for local businesses. Participants can look forward to engaging with industry experts, exploring innovative farming technologies, and enjoying a showcase of local maize products. Join us in celebrating Cross River's vibrant maize farming community and advancing agricultural excellence!
